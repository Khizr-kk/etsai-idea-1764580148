## Slide 2: Problem
**The Silent Epidemic in Outpatient Care**

*   **Diagnostic Errors:** A leading cause of patient harm, leading to delayed treatment, worsening conditions, and increased morbidity.
*   **Unnecessary Testing:** Over-ordering drives billions in waste, patient inconvenience, and potential complications from procedures.
*   **Clinician Burnout:** Primary care doctors are overwhelmed, managing complex cases with limited time and resources, leading to fatigue and errors.
*   **The Cost:** Suboptimal outcomes and wasted resources drain the healthcare system and impact patient trust.

## Slide 3: Solution
**DiagnosAI: Your Intelligent Diagnostic Co-Pilot**

An AI-powered Clinical Decision Support system for outpatient clinicians.
*   **AI-Powered Diagnostic Suggestions:** Ranked list of potential diagnoses with probabilities, analyzing all patient data.
*   **Smart Test Recommendations:** Suggests necessary tests and flags potentially unnecessary ones, reducing waste.
*   **Seamless EHR Integration:** Securely ingests patient data from existing Electronic Health Records.
*   **Intuitive Clinician Dashboard:** Presents clear, evidence-backed insights and rationale for AI suggestions.

**Result: Higher accuracy, greater efficiency, better patient outcomes.**
